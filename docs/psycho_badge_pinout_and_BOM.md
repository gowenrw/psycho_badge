# Psycho Badge Pinout and BOM

## Pinout

ESP32 PINS and Thier Connections on the Badge

NEOPIXEL Strings
* 27 - Neo String 1 - D10, D11, D12 - Defcon
* 14 - Neo String 2 - D20, D21, D22, D23, D24 - MOTEL

Tradional LEDs
* 22 - Built In LED
* 33 - D1 - FREE
* 32 - D2 - WIFI
* 26 - D31, D32 - Clouds
* 25 - D4 - Clouds
* 19 - D51, D52 - Mansion
* 12 - D6 - Room_Door

EINK Dev Board
* 23 - DevBd 3 - SDI
* 18 - DevBd 4 - SCK
* 5  - DevDb 5 - CS
* 17 - DevBd 6 - DS
* 16 - DevBd 7 - RES
* 34 - DevBd 8 - BSY

Capacitive Touch Pads
4  - TCH1 1 - Bats
2  - TCH1 2 - Satelite
15 - TCH1 3 - Car
13 - TCH1 4 - Knife
